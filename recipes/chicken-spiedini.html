Chicken Spiedini

Description

Chicken Spiedini is one of my favorites! Often found skewer-style, the chicken breast is normally grilled or breaded, cooked with olive oil, lemon, and garlic with various Italian seasonings. You'll have to try this one.

Ingredients
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Chicken Breasts
Steps
  1. For the marinade, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, parsley, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.
  2. Chop chicken into pieces and combine with marinade. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Mix the beaten egg into the marinated chicken.
  4. Combine breadcrumbs with parmesan, salt and pepper.
  5. Coat the chicken pieces in the seasoned breadcrumbs.
  6. Thread Chicken onto skewers.
  7. Arrange the chicken skewers on a baking tray and drizzle with olive oil before baking in a preheated oven. Or, shallow fry until gliden brown and cooked through.
