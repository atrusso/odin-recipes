Homemade Salsa

Description

One of the more tedious desserts out there, Baklava is not for the faint of heart to make. Done with proper time and preperation this dessert packs a decadent punch.

Ingredients
  • Phyllo pastry
  • Pistachios
  • Walnuts
  • Sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground cloves
  • Orange extract
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Lemon juice
  • Melted butter
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degress Fahrenheit
  2. In a pan, add sugar and water, heat while occassionally stirring until sugar is dissolved
  3. Stir in honey, orange extract, clove
  4. Bring mixture to boil, then simmer for 25 minutes
  5. Remove syrup from heat, add in lemon juice
  6. Syrup will thicken over time
  7. In a blender, chop pistachios, walnuts together
  8. In a mixing bowl, add chopped up nuts along with sugar, cinnamon, and clove
  9. Unwrap and unroll phyllo dough and place in between two damp towels
  10. Place one sheet of phyllo dough in high rimmed baking sheet, brush on some melted butter, repeat this process until 1/3 of phyllo dough has been used
  11. Place 1/2 the nut mixture across the top layer of phyllo dough
  12. Continue to layer another 1/3 of the phyllo dough
  13. Place the other 1/2 of the nut mixture across the top layer of phyllo dough
  14. Continue to layer the last 1/3 of the phyllo dough
  15. With a sharp knife, cut baklava into small triangular shapes
  16. Place baklava in oven and bake for 25-35 minutes or until gliden brown
  17. After baklava has been removed from oven, pour honey syrup over baklava
